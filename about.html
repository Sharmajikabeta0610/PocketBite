Our Story

PocketBite was born from a simple idea: to connect people who love fresh, delicious food with local bakeries that have surplus items at the end of the day.

Our Mission

We're on a mission to reduce food waste while making high-quality bakery items more accessible to everyone. By connecting local bakeries with food lovers, we create a win-win situation for both businesses and consumers.

Reduce Waste

Help local bakeries reduce food waste by connecting them with customers who appreciate their surplus items.

Support Local

Support local bakeries by helping them generate additional revenue from items that would otherwise go to waste.

Save Money

Enjoy high-quality bakery items at discounted prices while making a positive impact on your community.

Our Impact

Since our launch, we've helped local bakeries reduce food waste by thousands of pounds while providing affordable, delicious treats to our community.
